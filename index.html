About

Complex products, made clear

For 6+ years I've designed digital products for international markets — healthcare, AI, fintech, Web3 and consumer platforms. I specialize in complex user flows, product strategy and UX research, and in building design systems that keep working as a product grows. I've taken products from 0→1 and helped mature ones scale, working closely with founders, PMs and engineers along the way.

At Pink Elephant Group I built States of Mind from 0→1 — defining its structure, user journeys and design patterns — while designing provider discovery, verification, onboarding and assessment flows across the mental-health ecosystem. A redesigned acquisition funnel lifted qualified leads by 16%, and a reworked verification workflow cut cycle time from 7 days to 2.

At X Labs I designed JGGL, an AI/ML product for human functional-state analysis, running interviews, usability testing and competitor research that fed directly into feature prioritization. For QR Charge I rebuilt the core scan–rent–return flow and UI kit for a power-bank rental network live across European markets. Earlier, at Duck.design I reworked navigation and information architecture for Momoboard, Conduit and Clevmate, and at SENLA I simplified dense, map-heavy dashboards for enterprise and insurance workflows.

Alongside client work, I taught UX/UI at BELHARD Academy, mentoring new designers through portfolio projects and real product-design workflows.
