At Pink Elephant Group I built States of Mind from 0→1 — its structure, user journeys and design system — while designing provider discovery, verification, onboarding and assessment flows across the mental-health ecosystem. A redesigned acquisition funnel lifted qualified leads by 16%, and a reworked verification workflow cut cycle time from 7 days to 2.

At X Labs I designed JGGL, an AI/ML product for human functional-state analysis, running interviews, usability testing and competitor research that fed straight into the roadmap. For QR Charge I rebuilt the core scan–rent–return flow and UI kit for a power-bank network live across European markets. Earlier, at Duck.design I reworked navigation and information architecture for Momoboard, Conduit and Clevmate — including localizing UX for Korean users — and at SENLA I simplified dense, map-heavy dashboards for enterprise and insurance workflows.

Alongside client work, I taught UX/UI at BELHARD Academy, mentoring new designers through portfolio projects and real product-design workflows.
